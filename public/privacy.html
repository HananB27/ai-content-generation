Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information (email address, password)
  • Content you generate and create
  • Connected social media account tokens (TikTok, YouTube)
  • Usage data and analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process your content generation requests
  • Publish videos to your connected social media accounts
  • Send you technical notices and support messages

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use PostgreSQL databases hosted on Neon for data storage.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini AI: For content generation
  • TikTok API: For publishing videos to TikTok
  • YouTube API: For publishing videos to YouTube Shorts

5. Account Connections

When you connect your TikTok or YouTube accounts, we store access tokens securely to enable video publishing. You can disconnect these accounts at any time.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Disconnect connected social media accounts

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
